How to Play

Overview

Liar's Dice is a game of bluffing and deduction. Players secretly roll dice under cups, then take turns bidding on the total number of a specific face value across ALL players' dice combined.

Setup

  • 2-6 players, each starts with 5 dice
  • Each round, all players roll their dice secretly

Bidding

  • Players take turns making bids on how many of a specific face value exist across ALL dice on the table
  • A bid consists of a quantity and a face value (e.g., "three 4s")
  • Each bid must be higher than the previous: either a higher quantity of any face, or the same quantity of a higher face

Wild Ones

When wild ones are enabled, any die showing a 1 counts as whatever face value is being bid. This makes bids more likely to be true!

Challenging

  • Instead of raising the bid, you can call "Liar!"
  • All dice are revealed and counted
  • If the bid IS met (enough dice of that face exist), the challenger loses a die
  • If the bid is NOT met, the bidder loses a die

Elimination

  • The player who loses a die starts the next round's bidding
  • A player with zero dice is eliminated
  • Last player standing wins!
